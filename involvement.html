About Us

Community
Involvement

Southern Software is proud to give back to the communities we serve. As a faith-based, employee-owned company, serving others is at the core of who we are.

As a Christian-based, employee-owned company headquartered in Southern Pines, NC, we believe that our responsibility extends beyond delivering great software. We are committed to being active members of our community and supporting the people who keep our communities safe.

From sponsoring local events to supporting charitable organizations, our team takes pride in giving back. We recognize that the agencies we serve — law enforcement, fire, EMS, and local government — are the backbone of the communities we all call home.

Our involvement reflects our core values: faith, integrity, partnership, and service. We are grateful for the opportunity to work alongside the dedicated men and women who protect and serve every day.
