Our Solutions

Product Suites

🚔
Public Safety Suite

PS Suite

A comprehensive, ever-growing family of products that gives law enforcement the tools they need — from dispatch to analytics and everything in between.

  • CAD — Computer-Aided Dispatch with real-time mapping
  • RMS — Records Management System
  • JMS — Jail Management System
  • Mobile Communication & Field Unit Integration
  • Narcotics Management & Quarter Master
  • Data Analytics & Clery Compliance Center
  • Citizen Connect — Public-Facing Data Portal

Financial Management Suite

FMS Suite

10 user-configurable modules that can be used individually or integrated together for a complete, powerful Financial Management System.

  • General Ledger & Budget Management
  • Accounts Payable & Receivable
  • Payroll & Human Resources
  • Purchase Orders & Inventory Control
  • Fixed Assets & Project Accounting
  • Utility Billing — Citi-Pak
  • Fully configurable by end users
