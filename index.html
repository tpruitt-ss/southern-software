Public Safety Suite

PS Suite

A comprehensive, ever-growing family of products that gives law enforcement the tools they need — from dispatch to analytics and everything in between.

  • CAD — Computer-Aided Dispatch with real-time mapping
  • RMS — Records Management System
  • JMS — Jail Management System
  • Mobile Communication & Field Unit Integration
  • Narcotics Management & Quarter Master
  • Data Analytics & Clery Compliance Center
  • Citizen Connect — Public-Facing Data Portal

FMS Suite
